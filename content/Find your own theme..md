# Find your own theme

I came across this [unixporn setup](https://www.reddit.com/r/unixporn/comments/1rbh1uf/sway_using_gnuplot_as_desktop_ornamentation/) and it reminded me of something I keep noticing:

the stuff that feels the most memorable usually has a real theme behind it.

Not just "good design" in the generic sense, but an actual point of view.

That is what I want with my own work too. I do not want stuff that just looks clean. I want it to feel like it came from somewhere specific.

That is a big part of what I am trying to do with [[Quartz]] right now. Not just make it usable, but make it feel like *my* thing.
