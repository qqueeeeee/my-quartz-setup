# Lumina Invest

Lumina Invest was a portfolio dashboard project I built with a friend who actively trades stocks.

The goal was to make something that felt closer to a real market dashboard instead of a boring spreadsheet-looking tracker.

## What I remember mattering here

- getting CSV import flows to work properly
- handling awkward broker export formats
- making market data and holdings feel clean instead of messy
- building something that was actually useful to a real person, not just me
