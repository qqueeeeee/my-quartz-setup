# PathForge AI

PathForge AI was a hackathon project I built around career planning and resume mentoring.

The idea was to make it feel less like a boring career portal and more like an actual AI guide that could help someone figure out where they are, where they want to go, and what to do next.

## What made it interesting

- it had a stronger concept than just "upload resume and get tips"
- it pushed me into quick product decisions under time pressure
- it mixed AI, frontend work, backend work, and presentation thinking all in one thing

## Stack / direction

- FastAPI backend
- React frontend
- Groq models for inference
- roadmap / resume / interview style features
