# TCS Ion NQT prep plan

This note is basically a lock-in plan for the TCS Ion NQT.

## Notes

The TCS iON NQT prep problem is not just "study hard."

It is really three problems at once:

- clear the aptitude / verbal / coding parts of the exam
- turn whatever result comes out of that into interview momentum
- use the prep period to build a stronger overall SDE profile instead of treating it like an isolated exam

The practical prep split was:

- aptitude practice through repeated pattern exposure
- coding practice in [[Python]] with awareness of TCS-specific compiler quirks
- interview prep built around projects, DSA basics, and common CS fundamentals

The bigger takeaway is that exam prep like this works much better when it plugs into a longer career plan.

So instead of treating NQT as the whole game, it made more sense to use it as one checkpoint while also improving:

- my resume
- my project framing
- my DSA level
- my interview answers around [[SelfForge]], [[Lumina Invest]], and the rest of my work
