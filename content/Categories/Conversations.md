## Main hubs

## Why these are here

A lot of these chats capture project decisions, career thinking, tooling rabbit holes, and ideas that never made it into the vault properly the first time around.

So instead of leaving them trapped in JSON, I turned them into actual notes with tags, themes, and links back into the rest of the graph.

The notes themselves live in the root now, same as most of the rest of the vault. This page is just the overview.
