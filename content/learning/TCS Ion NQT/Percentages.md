Formula 1: 
Calculating Percentage Change when initial and final values are given:  
$$ 
\frac{Absolute Value Change} {Original Quantity} * 100
$$
Example: X scored 80 marks in a test while she scored 50 in the first one. What's the percentage change in Amara's score?
